Composer Bio:

Kandis Taylor began her career by earning her Bachelors Degree in Music Education and Masters Degree in Percussion Performance from Brigham Young University. She has over 15 years experience teaching in public schools, directing and adjudicating bands and percussion ensembles at the junior high and high school level. While teaching in California, she received the "Teacher of the Year" award in 2015 after teaching at her school for only three years. She recently received the 2019 “Accent in Excellence" Award from the Alpine School District as Director of Bands & Percussion Ensembles at Lakeridge Junior High in Orem, Utah. She is actively involved in industry organizations including the Utah Music Educators Association, Utah Bandmasters Association, and Percussive Arts Society. When she isn’t teaching public school, Kandis composes music and has several compositions published through C.A. Publications. Taylor.
